Behold, combat rules. Plenty of people love to fight in D&D for some reason, but most of those people don't even know how it works. This should give you a little rundown of the common things you'll be doing in every combat. That would be moving, taking actions, bonus actions, and reactions as well as examples of each. There are plenty of other things you can do too, but lets keep it simple for now.
