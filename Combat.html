Beginners Guide to Dungeon and Dragons

Rule Explanations
Examples
  • Movement: Each character will have a movement speed determined by race, and sometimes modified by other methods. Each turn one can move up to that distance for free, on top of anything else they want to do. Gotta get close to the goblin if you want to stab them. You can also increase this movement for the turn by taking the Dash Action.
  • Actions: Each person has 1 action, and this is used to do the majority of things on your turn. This could range from chugging a potion, wacking em with your sword, or casting a spell. There are plenty of things that do not require your action but, for the most part, it will always be the biggest thing you do on each of your turns.
  • Bonus Action: There are some things that are specifically called bonus actions. Don't worry, these are only entirely different from your normal action and sometimes you won't even have anything helpful to do with it. This tends to be for more minor things or even spells that are supposed to be particularly fast.
  • Reactions: These are things you can do when it's not even your turn. Normally it will just be trying to hit someone when they walk away from you in the middle of a fight like a loser, but there are some things that can increase what you can do with reactions. Just like with Actions and Bonus Actions. You only get 1 though until your turn starts again, so it's not like you can play on everyone elses turn and yours.
  • "Looks like the enemy is 20ft away. Good thing I have 30ft of movement each turn. Now I can freely walk over to them and start attacking with my spear on the same turn."
  • "After moving towards the goblin, I would like to use my action to hit them with my sword."
  • "I've already used my action to stab that goblin with my spear, darn. Oh wait, I can cast this spell 'Healing Word' as a bonus action because it's so fast and says so right on the sheet. Don't worry Larry, here's a little healing."
  • "Hey look, that goblin is trying to run from me in the middle of this fight, let me stab them with my sword because they did not 'Disengage.' Wait, another one is trying to do the same thing?! If only I had another reaction. Gotta wait for my next turn to come around so I can really show em."
